What is CRAB AGENT?

CRAB AGENT is an AI-assisted market intelligence scanner for Solana meme coins. It continuously observes real market activity from DexScreener, scores each token it finds using the CRAB SCORE engine, and surfaces the highest-quality opportunities first - so you don't have to manually scan hundreds of pairs yourself.

What is CRAB SCORE?

CRAB SCORE is a 0-100 number computed from a combination of indicators: momentum across multiple timeframes, liquidity, trading volume and turnover, valuation (FDV), liquidity backing, holder data (when available), trade frequency, and buy/sell pressure. The final recommendation also depends on Signal Strength (confidence) and a penalty for overextended or risky conditions - not the score alone.

These are the real thresholds used by the engine - reaching STRONG BUY or BUY also requires a minimum Signal Strength and a low enough risk penalty, so a high score alone doesn't guarantee the top tier. CRAB SCORE is not a price prediction - it's a structured read of current market conditions, not a guarantee of future performance.

What is Strength?

Strength (shown as Signal Strength, or Confidence internally) reflects how much the engine actually trusts the signal it just computed - based on how complete the available data is and how consistently different timeframes agree with each other. A high score with low Strength means the underlying data is thin or contradictory, and the recommendation should be treated with more caution.

What is Early / Mid / Late?

This is CRAB AGENT's estimate of entry timing, separate from the score itself. It looks at how young the token is, how extended the current price move already is, and whether momentum is still building or already fading, to estimate whether an opportunity is still early, already in the middle of its move, or likely late to enter.

Data Sources

Every number in CRAB AGENT comes from DexScreener's free public API - liquidity, volume, market cap, FDV, and transaction data are pulled directly from live pairs. Discovery itself is market-driven, combining several real DexScreener feeds rather than a fixed keyword list:

  • Boosted tokens (latest and top)
  • Recently updated and newly created token profiles
  • Community takeovers
  • Trending narratives/categories (DexScreener's own trending computation)

The engine combines all of these sources, removes duplicates, and scores every unique token it finds - a keyword search box exists separately for manually looking up a specific token, but it never drives automatic discovery.

Every cycle, CRAB AGENT observes all candidates it can find across its market sources, removes anything that fails basic quality checks (extremely low liquidity, near-zero volume, obvious shell pools), runs the CRAB SCORE engine on everything that qualifies, and displays the top-ranked opportunities first.
